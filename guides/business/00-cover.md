## What Makes Business Edition Different

The Personal Edition teaches you to build an AI assistant. The Business Edition teaches you to build an **AI-powered business operation**.

The difference:
- **Personal** connects 5 APIs → **Business** connects 14 with a reusable toolchain
- **Personal** picks one model → **Business** routes tasks to the optimal model automatically
- **Personal** runs locally → **Business** deploys to a VPS for 24/7 uptime
- **Personal** sends alerts → **Business** executes trades, sends emails, processes payments
- **Personal** remembers conversations → **Business** maintains active project context, task queues, and credential vaults

This is the edition for people who are serious about building systems that generate value autonomously.

Let's build something real.
