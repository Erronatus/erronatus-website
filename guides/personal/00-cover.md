## Before You Begin

**Prerequisites:**
- A computer running Windows, macOS, or Linux
- Node.js 20 or higher installed
- A Telegram or Discord account
- An API key from at least one AI provider (OpenRouter recommended)
- 2-4 hours of focused time

**What You'll Build:**
By the end of this guide, you will have a fully operational AI assistant running on your machine that you can talk to through Telegram or Discord. It will remember your conversations, execute tasks on schedule, and connect to real-world services through APIs.
