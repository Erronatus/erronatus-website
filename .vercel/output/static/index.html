The Framework

Five Layers.
One Autonomous System.

Each chapter builds on the last. By the end, you have a fully operational AI workforce running on your own infrastructure.

01

Infrastructure

Deploy OpenClaw on your machine. Configure your AI gateway, set up Telegram or Discord as your command interface, and establish the foundation for autonomous operations.

02

Engine Routing

Configure multi-model AI routing. Free models for simple tasks, mid-tier for coding, premium for strategic thinking. Your system automatically picks the cheapest capable engine.

03

API Integration

Connect 14+ services into a single reusable toolchain. Trading APIs, databases, deployment platforms, email systems, and analytics — all wired up and ready to automate.

04

Memory Systems

Give your AI persistent memory. Daily logs, long-term memory files, credential vaults, and context management — your AI remembers everything across sessions.

05

Automation

Build cron jobs, heartbeat monitors, and autonomous workflows. Your AI checks emails, watches markets, sends alerts, and executes tasks — on schedule, without supervision.
